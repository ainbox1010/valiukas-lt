## AccessGuard

AccessGuard is a custom-built web application designed to manage access control and operational workflows in factory environments.

It enables administrators to define zones, assign permissions, monitor employee movements, and manage vehicle access — all through a centralized platform.

### Core Capabilities

- Definition of workplace zones and role-based access permissions  
- Access card creation and assignment  
- Reader configuration for permission validation  
- Real-time dashboard showing current zone activity  
- Monitoring of employee presence and movement  

### Reporting & Analytics

- Detailed access event logs  
- Time spent in specific zones  
- Attendance and idle time tracking  
- Automated activity report generation  

### Extended Logistics Functionality

- Truck scheduling and cargo coordination  
- Driver and vehicle registration  
- Unique request numbers for incoming trucks  
- Terminal queue notification system  
- Recording of loading details (weight, products, completion time)  

### Impact

- Reduced unauthorized access  
- Improved oversight and permission management  
- Real-time operational transparency  
- Increased security and workforce coordination  

AccessGuard strengthens workplace security while streamlining both personnel and vehicle movement management.
