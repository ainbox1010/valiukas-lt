## Accounting Firm — Tax & Reporting Automation

A technology-driven accounting firm sought to reduce manual effort in recurring regulatory and accounting workflows.

Accounting processes are well-structured and rule-based, making them ideal candidates for automation.

### Solution

Delivered by **erobot.ai**, the automation included:

- Depreciation recalculation in accounting software  
- Automated downloading of cross-check reports from tax systems  
- Formation, review, and submission of regulatory reports  
- Integration with electronic signature workflows  

### Impact

- Significant time savings  
- Reduced human error in financial reporting  
- Improved process reliability  
- Increased employee focus on higher-value work  
- Strong foundation for continued robotization  

This case illustrates how structured accounting environments can benefit significantly from robotic process automation.
