## Logistics Company — OCR & Shipping Document Automation

A freight forwarding and ship agency company needed to streamline the processing of shipping documentation.

Shipping paperwork such as CMRs, invoices, and packing lists was handled manually, creating delays and inefficiencies.

### Solution

Delivered by **erobot.ai**, the system:

- Uses OCR and machine learning to extract data from shipping PDFs  
- Automatically digitizes document content  
- Transfers structured information directly into transport management software  
- Reduces manual document handling  

### Impact

- Near-instant document processing  
- Reduced administrative workload  
- Faster internal operations  
- Improved service speed for customers  

This case demonstrates how AI-based document processing can significantly accelerate logistics workflows.
