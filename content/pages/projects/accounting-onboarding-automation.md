## Accounting Firm — Customer Onboarding Automation

An accounting and tax advisory firm needed to streamline the onboarding of new clients.

Previously, onboarding required significant manual data entry and document preparation by staff.

### Solution

Delivered by **erobot.ai**, the automation:

- Sends structured online questionnaires to new clients  
- Collects company data and authorized representative information  
- Automatically generates required documents (contracts, powers of attorney, etc.)  
- Sends documents for signing  
- Allows easy updates of existing documentation  

Employees only intervene when data is incomplete or requires clarification.

### Impact

- Immediate time savings  
- Faster onboarding process  
- Reduced administrative errors  
- Improved customer experience  
- Scalable solution for firms managing large volumes of standardized documents  

This case illustrates how onboarding automation can significantly improve operational efficiency in professional service firms.
