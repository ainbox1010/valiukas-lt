## Collaboration Model

I architect and design AI-enabled systems and typically lead system architecture, workflow automation design, and AI integration. Some projects require specialized delivery teams, industry experience, or additional implementation capacity.

For such cases, I collaborate with trusted partners.  
Projects listed on this site clearly indicate whether they were delivered directly by me or in cooperation with a partner.

Some projects are anonymized where confidentiality agreements apply.
